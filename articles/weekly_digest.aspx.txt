How to submit

To submit a calendar event for consideration for the Weekly Digest, post the event on the YSM calendar and be sure to use the "+" button to the right in "Popular Calendars" to the right of Weekly Digest option

Subscribe

The Weekly Digest is distributed to School of Medicine faculty, students, and staff each Friday morning by email. Anyone is welcome to join the list. If you are not already receiving it and would like to subscribe, visit the University Messaging Service subscriptions page.
